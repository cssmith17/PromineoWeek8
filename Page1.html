-- Date Released -- -- Developer -- -- Director -- -- Genre --
November 17th, 2004 Konami Hideo Kojima Stealth, Action
  • Metal Gear Solid 3: Snake Eater, despite being the fifth installment in the Metal Gear video game series, is a prequel to the series at large.
  • Set in 1964, a CIA special operations operative only known as Naked Snake, is sent covertly into Russia to exfiltrate a russian
    scientist Nikolai Sokolov, sabotage Sokolov's experimental superweapon; the Shagohod, and assassinate his no defected former mentor, only known as The Boss.
  • Considered one of the greatest video games of all time, this game combines a fascinating and complex plot with entertaining sneaking and shooting gameplay missions.
